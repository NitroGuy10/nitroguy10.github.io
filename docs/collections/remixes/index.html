Sharks & Skybreak & Paper Skies - Trinite

(The Sass Remix)

Released: 14 May 2023

Genre: Complextro / Progressive House

Some vibe-y, multi-genre madness in this remix. Also maybe my record for most lead solos in a song. I've been a huge fan of Sharks, Skybreak, and Paper Skies for a long time now and their musical styles have had a big influence on me as a producer. These three are constantly pushing the limits in this scene and I'm super glad to have been able to remix a track made by all three of them! <3

Fishcracks - Accusations

(The Sass Remix)

Released: 18 August 2022

Genre: Melodic Dubstep

A fun but melancholy remix in a UK dubstep-inspired style. I tried to step away from the crazy complex lead solos and frenzied arrangement I usually go for and I'm very happy I did. This one came together extremely quickly, and the result is a laid-back and emotional tune with a strong riff-based approach that I ended up really liking. Hope you enjoy!
