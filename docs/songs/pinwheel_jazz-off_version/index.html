The Sass

Pinwheel (Jazz-off Version)

Released: 13 February 2023

Genre: Jazztronica

Cover Artist: @amaryllis_no

Collection: Remixes

The full title of this one is "Skybreak, Atura & THREESIXTY - Pinwheel (Jazz-off Version) [feat. PlexusPlay, The Sass, CyberScythe]". I was one of the three winners of the Pinwheel solo contest held by Skybreak, Atura, and THREESIXTY. My solo is the second one in the track! This was a super fun contest resulting in a super fun track. You all should go check out the other winners, as well as the original artists!
