The Sass

Endless (ft. Vishnu)

Released: 25 June 2020

Genre: Melodic Dubstep

Collection: Candy Fractals EP

A somber but powerful melodic dubstep tune. This is another one of those songs that went through a MASSIVE rework to get to where it is now. This particular one was in 4/4, in the key of C minor, and was going to have a normie progressive house drop. When I was itching to try making dubstep, I decided to flip this song and I'm so happy I did! Also, huge thanks to my buddy Vishnu for the sax solo!
