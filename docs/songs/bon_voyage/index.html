The Sass

Bon Voyage

Released: 5 August 2017

Genre: Orchestral

Cover Artist: Me

Collection: Older Tracks

My last fully orchestral song. I had actually come up with this melody when I was still making songs with midi. The first phrase was played by a pan flute and the second by a brass section. Once I starting using FL, I decided to recreate it and finally finish it.
