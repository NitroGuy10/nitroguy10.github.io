The Sass

Hurricane

Released: 28 July 2016

Genre: Orchestral

Collection: Older Tracks

Hurricane. Oh Hurricane. I have soooooo many memories of this song from writing it, to showing it to my music teacher, to having my friends play it in class, to having my mom force me to play it for my grandmother. This song is a journey. I was heavily inspired by the kind of music we were playing in school band at the time.
